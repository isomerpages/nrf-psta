### **Celebrating the Pinnacle of Science & Technology in Singapore**


For 10 years, the <b>President’s Science and Technology Awards (PSTA)</b> have recognised individuals with innovative ideas who have made exceptional contributions to Singapore. Over 60 men and women have been singled out for their spirit o f innovation. Their passion and talent for science and technology have inspired the scientific community and helped t o shape Singapore’s research, innovation and enterprise landscape. Who are they and what do the y have to say on the future of science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) in Singapore?
